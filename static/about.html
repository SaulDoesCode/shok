Welcome to kurshok.space, a new web platform for philosophical content and paid posts. kurshok.space is more than just a website, it strives to be a community of thinkers, writers, and readers who share a passion for exploring meaningful and interesting thinkers, ideas, and concepts.

kurshok.space is:

  • Powered by Rust, a fast and reliable programming language that ensures high performance and security. Rust also enables us to create social features akin to Twitter, such as search, timelines, likes, and reposting. You can follow your favorite authors, interact with their posts, and discover new perspectives on various topics.
  • A publishing platform, that will enable people monetize their content with points on the website that can be purchased/fundged from the admin via negotiable means including crypto. You can create your own articles and share your thoughts with the world. You can also read articles from other people and learn from their experiences, or pay to access articles that others/the-admin have/has made and priced.
  • This is a place where you can express yourself freely, learn from others, and grow as a person. Whether you are interested in ethics, metaphysics, logic, or anything in between, you will find something that sparks your curiosity and challenges your mind.
Privacy Policy:
We do not collect any personal information from you. We do not use cookies or any other tracking technology. We do not share your data with third parties. We do not sell your data to advertisers. We do not store your data on our servers. We do not store your data on your device. We do not store your data anywhere.
